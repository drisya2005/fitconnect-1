⚙️ How the Allocation Works

1

Choose a plan

Purchase Basic (≤₹600), Standard (≤₹1000), or Premium (₹1000+). Your balance = monthlyFee × months.

2

Check in at any partner gym

Choose the gym from the partner list and check in. The session timer starts.

3

Automatic fee deduction

Formula: (monthlyFee / 4.33) × gymMultiplier × (days / 7) is deducted from balance.

4

Switch gyms anytime

3 weeks at one gym, 1 week at another — balance is fairly split across both.

🌱 Beginner Corner

📅 3-Day Starter Plan

  • Day 1 — Strength: Squats, rows, push-ups, plank
  • Day 2 — Cardio: 25 min treadmill / cycling
  • Day 3 — Mobility: Yoga, crunches, breathing

✅ First-Day Gym Tips

  • Arrive 15 min early, tour the facility
  • Ask staff for equipment demos
  • Start light — form beats weight
  • Bring water, towel, locker lock

🏋️ Gym Etiquette

  • Re-rack weights after use
  • No phone calls on the gym floor
  • Don't monopolize equipment
  • Wipe down machines after use

🔔 Stay Consistent

  • Set a fixed gym time daily
  • Track every workout in FitConnect
  • Log BMI monthly for real progress
  • Aim for 3 sessions/week minimum
