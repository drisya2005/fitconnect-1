### Project Description
[The Smart Gym Membership Application allows users to use different partner gyms with one single membership based on the fee category they choose. It also helps users track their workouts and provides simple guidance and reminders to maintain a healthy lifestyle.
]

### The Problem statement
[Traditional gym memberships are limited to a single location, which is inconvenient for people who frequently travel.
Users often have to pay for multiple memberships when shifting between places.
Beginners also face challenges in getting proper guidance and tracking their fitness progress.
]

### The Solution
[We created a Smart Gym Membership Application that allows users to access multiple gyms with a single membership.
The app tracks workouts, calories, and progress while providing beginner-friendly guidance and reminders.
It also adjusts membership fees based on gym usage, making fitness flexible, affordable, and easy to follow.
]
